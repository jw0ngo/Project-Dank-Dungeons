### 2026-06-09 — Record taste as you make it, in your own words

- **Principle:** A creative call that isn't written down — in the director's own phrasing — can't be
  served consistently by other agents or learned by a future one.
- **Why:** From Dust is AI-native; the studio compounds only through documentation. Taste is the
  hardest thing to infer after the fact and the most valuable to capture verbatim.
- **How to apply:** Log every real call to the Direction Log in `../CREATIVE_MANIFESTO.md` as it
  happens — the feeling chased and the reasoning, not just the verdict.
